About Me.
Hey there, my name is Abi!

I'm a 23 year old frontend web developer. I studied Computer Science at Coventry University and this is where my love for coding evolved.

I've always enjoyed being creative - whether it's doing someone's makeup or drawing in my sketchbook but merging technology with creativity just takes it to a whole new level! What I enjoy the most about web development is watching my ideas come to life on the screen.

The motto I live by when developing websites is ''Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication'' because in my opinion, less is more.

Fun fact about me: When I'm not coding, you'll most likely find me in the gym.
